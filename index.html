About Me

I am a 4th year Ph.D. student at Heriot-Watt University. My research focuses on spatial econometrics, machine learning, panel data, and time series analysis. In particular, I am currently working on developing a new method to estimate an unknown spatial weights matrix which could be asymmetric by combining CCE (Pesaran, 2006) and OCMT (Chudik et al., 2018). The work includes theoretical proof, Monte Carlo simulations and an application to world enegyt data. I also work on combining different algorithms using an ensemble method named "Stacking" to achieve better prediction accuracy, and apply the method to real world forecasting such as GDP and energy prices.
